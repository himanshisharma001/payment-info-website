About Us

At Sikhwal Multiservices Pvt. Ltd., we believe in transforming everyday financial and essential services into seamless, secure, and accessible solutions for everyone — from bustling city businesses to remote rural communities. Headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan, we are a dynamic and forward-thinking company offering a wide range of digital financial services, utility solutions, and travel booking facilities under one trusted roof.

Founded with a mission to bridge the digital divide and empower both individuals and enterprises, we have built a reputation for speed, reliability, and innovation. Whether it’s AEPS transactions, Domestic Money Transfers, PAN Card services, or instant bill payments, we offer our customers and agents a one-stop platform that’s simple to use and powerfully efficient.

What sets us apart is our "Unbreakable Bond of Trust" — a commitment to transparency, customer satisfaction, and long-term partnerships. Our collaboration with leading and trusted financial platforms ensures that every transaction is encrypted, swift, and secure. From distributing POS machines to enabling QR/UPI solutions for merchants, we empower local retailers, distributors, and individuals to become digitally active and financially independent.

We serve a vast and diverse clientele including businesses, local shop owners, students, travelers, professionals, and service agents across India. Our services are designed to save time, maximize value, and deliver convenience, all while ensuring regulatory compliance and customer data security.

At Sikhwal Multiservices, we don't just offer services — we deliver solutions that simplify lives. We’re proud to be a part of India’s digital growth journey and remain committed to expanding our offerings, reaching new milestones, and building lasting relationships rooted in trust, speed, and service excellence.
